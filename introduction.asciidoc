= Introduction

I have created this repository to provide modelers information that they may find useful to build and convert thier models. I mostly refered to Italeri (and Revell) 1/24 scale plastic models (because this is my personal main modeling interest) but most information will be relevant to any scale.

Personally I think that every truck looks much better with 3 or more axles. Unfortunatly, Italeri (and Revell) offers most of their European trucks only as 4x2 tractor heads. 

As mentioned above my main interest is  designing suspensions (anything that is not 4x2) to modify Italeri and other manufacturers 1/24 scale kits. Therefore, the information on this site will be highly skewed to this direction but I will add any piece of information that I think is interesting (at least to me) or that may be useful to a truck modeler.
