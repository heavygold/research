== Introduction

== Marks

[id=m1,reftext=Mk. 1]
=== Mark 1

1973 -> 1988

NG (New Generation) range

[id=m2,reftext=Mk. 2]
=== Mark 2

1989 -> 1998

SK (Schwere Klasse = Heavy Class) range

Visual changes compare to the NG range:
* Bottoms of side windows are slopped forward (on the NG cab they are horizontal)
* Larger vertical door handles
* Full width front grille
* Larger, more complex, dashboard (I have designed the correct dashboard in 1/24. It is available on Cults3D - link:https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/game/mercedes-sk-updated-parts-1-24[Mercedes SK updated parts 1/24])

[id=m3a,reftext=Mk. 3a]
=== Mark 3a

1996 -> 2003

Actros (later known as MP1)

Completely new truck

[id=m3b,reftext=Mk. 3b]
=== Mark 3b

2003 -> 2008

Actros MP2

Larger grille, more "flowing" headlights.

[id=m3c,reftext=Mk. 3c]
=== Mark 3c

2008 -> 2011

Actors MP3

Modified grille, side mirrors mounted closer to the cab sides.

[id=m4a,reftext=Mk. 4a]
=== Mark 4a

2011 -> 2018

The New Actors. Also known as MP4.

[id=m4b,reftext=Mk. 4b]
=== Mark 4b

2018 -> present

Facelift. Also known as MP5.

== Kits

Most of Mercedes marks were\are offered by Italeri. All as tractors and the older marks also as rigids. Marks <<m1>> and <<m2>> were offered as 6x4 tractors and ridigs but mark <<3a>> and forward were offered only as a 4x2 tractors.
